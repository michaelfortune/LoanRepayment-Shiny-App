Introduction

This application is designed to calculate loan repayments for a given loan amount and interest rate. Many people do not realise that in order to reduce the loan period significantly, a modest increase in monthly is all that is required. This shiny application allows people to do just that. You begin by selecting the intersset rate, amount of the loan and a typical repayment period. This will calculate the monthly repayments and the total repayment over the chosen period. You can then adjust the repayment period down or up and instantly see the difference. By adjusting the values, one can easily see what the effect can be i.e how much you might save, or how much more you might be able to borrow if a longer repayment period is chosen.

Technology

The app is known as a shiny app and is hosted at shinyapps.io, by the people who developed RStudio. In terms of code structure, there are effectively two parts: a user interface (ui.R) and a server part (server.R). The ui provide the mechanism for entering the values, while the server part does the calculating of the payment and the rendering of the graphical output.
